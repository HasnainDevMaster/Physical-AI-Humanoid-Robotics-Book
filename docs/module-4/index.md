# Module 4: Humanoid Robot Development

## Overview

This module focuses on the development of humanoid robots, which are robots with human-like characteristics and capabilities. Humanoid robots represent one of the most challenging and fascinating areas of robotics, requiring integration of multiple complex systems including locomotion, balance, manipulation, and interaction. This module covers the unique challenges and solutions in humanoid robot development.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will:
- Understand the principles of humanoid robot kinematics and dynamics
- Know how to design and implement walking and balance algorithms
- Be proficient in humanoid robot control systems
- Understand the challenges of humanoid robot perception and interaction
- Be familiar with advanced humanoid robot platforms
- Know how to integrate all previous concepts in a humanoid robot system

## Topics Covered

1. Introduction to Humanoid Robotics
2. Humanoid Robot Kinematics
3. Walking and Balance Algorithms
4. Control Systems for Humanoid Robots
5. Human-Robot Interaction
6. Advanced Humanoid Platforms
7. Integration of All Course Concepts

## Prerequisites

Before starting this module, you should have:
- Understanding of ROS2 fundamentals (Module 1)
- Knowledge of simulation techniques (Module 2)
- Experience with NVIDIA Isaac platform (Module 3)
- Understanding of kinematics and dynamics
- Basic knowledge of control systems
